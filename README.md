  <li>Style: Cartoon</li>
  <li>Genre: Physics-based, puzzle-solving Game </li>
  <li>Gameplay: The player needs to control a waterdrop to perform different tasks in multiple levels </li>

   # Game Design
   <li> Level 1: Originating in a kitchen, a waterdrop exhibits high fluidity and gradually evaporates. It ceases to exist before its water level reaches zero and can be replenished by colliding with the blue cubes. Upon contact with the sugar cube inside the purple jar, the waterdrop undergoes a transformation, acquiring viscosity and 'evolving' into a soft body. </li>
  <li> Level 2: The waterdrop exits through a doorway and enters a city, now equipped with grapple and swing abilities. It employs these newfound skills to gather 10 AI-controlled energy cubes in order to progress </li>
